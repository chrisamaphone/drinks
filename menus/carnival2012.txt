Sweet
-----
Bee's Knees -- gin, lime, honey -- like a kiss from a bee!
    i: 2 gin, 1/2 lime juice, barspoon honey
    g: lemon wheel
Amaroni -- gin, sweet vermouth, averna -- nutty caramel and herbs
    i: 1 gin, 1 sweet vermouth, 1 averna
    g: orange peel
Mojito -- rum, lime, simple, mint -- refreshing old-school classic
    i: 2-3 oz rum, 1 oz lime, 1/2 simple, 8-10 mint leaves
    p: muddle simple, mint, splash soda in fizz glass; squeeze lime halves, leave hull
    g: lime wheel

    simple, mint, splash of soda in glass -- muddle
    squeeze lime halves, leave hull
    add rum, stir, fill with ice
    top with soda water

Sour
----
Blackstrap Sour -- blackstrap rum, lime, falernum, angostura (lime squeeze) -- buttery and rich
    i: 2 blackstrap rum, 1 lime, 1/2 falernum, dash or two angostura
    g: lime squeeze
*Pisco Sour -- pisco, lemon, simple, egg white, angostura -- creamy and herbaceous
    i: 2 pisco, 1 lemon, 1/2 simple, dash or two angostura
    g: lemon twist
Applejack Sour -- applejack, lime, simple (lime wheel) -- like a crisp granny smith
    i: 2 applejack, 1 lime, 1/2 simple
    g: lime wheel

Bitter
------
Irish Negroni -- irish whiskey, dubonnet, cynar -- bittersweet and complex [var: Negroni, Mesoclassiconi, etc.]
    i: 1 irish whiskey, 1 dubonnet, 1 cynar
    g: orange wheel
Jasmine -- gin, lemon, cointreau, campari -- grapefruit without the grapefruit
    i: 1-1/2 gin, 3/4 lemon, 1/4 cointreau, 1/4 campari
    g: lemon twist
Americano -- campari, sweet vermouth, seltzer -- refreshingly bitter and appetizing [var: several]
    i: 2 campari, 1 sweet vermouth, top with seltzer
    g: orange wheel

Herbal
------
Bijou -- gin, sweet vermouth, chartreuse -- like a gift from god [var: Bitter]
    i: 1-1/2 gin, 1/2 sweet vermouth, 1/2 green chartreuse
    g: lemon twist
Green-Eyed Monster -- irish whiskey, sweet vermouth, absinthe, angostura -- bracingly anise; don't be afraid
    i: 2 irish whiskey, 1/2 sweet vermouth, 1/4 absinthe, dash angostura
    g: lemon twist
Creole #2 -- bourbon, curacao, angostura, peychaud's, with an herbsaint rinse -- bouillabaise not included [var: Sazerac]
    i: 2 bourbon, 1/2 curacao, dash angostura, dash peychaud's
    p: herbsaint rinse
    g: orange peel

Floral
------
Aviation -- gin, lemon, maraschino, violette -- sky blue house favorite [var: Necessity, Sensation]
    i: 1-1/2 gin, 3/4 lemon, 1/2 maraschino, 1/4 violette
    g: lemon twist
*Ramos Gin Fizz -- gin, lemon, lime, simple, egg white, cream, orange flower water -- creamy, foamy new orleans classic [var: other fizzes, Golden Fizz]
    i: 2 gin, 1/2 lemon, 1/2 lime, 1/2 simple, 1 cream, 1 egg white, 5 drops orange flower water
    p: froth all ingredients together with no ice to texture egg; then shake, top with soda
    g: lemon twist
Ninth Ward -- bourbon, lime, falernum, st. germain, peychaud's -- boston/new orleans fusion neoclassic
    i: 1-1/2 bourbon, 3/4 lime, 3/4 falernum, 1/2 st. germain, dash or two peychaud's
    g: orange wheel

* contains raw egg ingredients (consuming raw or undercooked egg can be delicious)
