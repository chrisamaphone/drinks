Bee's Knees

2 oz gin
1/2 oz lime
barspoon honey (about 1/4 oz)

Try to stir honey in before shaking.  Garnish: lemon wheel (like a bee!)

Made 03-11-2011 for Chris.  Kind of like a Daiquiri, but with gin and honey
instead of rum and simple syrup.  Barspoon!

Featured at Womcave Carnival Cocktails 2012, 04-21-2012.
