Camparbaro, or
Zucca Camparbaro, or
Camparbaratif

(an aperitif based on Campari and Rabarbaro Zucca..
 it doesn't really have a name...
 we all know how to make it in Italy...)

1 oz Campari
1 oz Rabarbaro Zucca
Prosecco
1 orange wheel
1 lime wedge
1 sprig mint (8-10 leaves)

Slice orange wheel and lime wedge in half, and muddle in mixing tin with mint.
Add Campari and Rabarbaro and shake with ice.  Pour into a chilled cocktail
glass, top with prosecco, and garnish with a squozen orange wheel.

The perfect aperitif!
